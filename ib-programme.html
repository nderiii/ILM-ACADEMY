The International Baccalaureate at ILM Academy

At ILM Academy, we are proud to be International Baccalaureate (IB) candidate school, a globally recognized educational framework that fosters critical thinking, international-mindedness, and a holistic approach to learning. The IB programmes are designed to develop inquiring, knowledgeable, and caring young people who are motivated to succeed and contribute to a better, more peaceful world through intercultural understanding and respect.

Our commitment to the IB philosophy aligns seamlessly with ILM Academy's vision of nurturing students with keen minds, exemplary character, and the ability to innovate, lead, and mentor. The IB curriculum, utilized by over 5,500 schools worldwide, provides a coherent educational experience that emphasizes intellectual, personal, emotional, and social growth.

Global Recognition

IB qualifications are recognized and highly respected by leading universities worldwide, providing students with exceptional international opportunities.

Holistic Development

The IB emphasizes intellectual, personal, emotional, and social growth, developing well-rounded individuals prepared for university and life beyond.

International-Mindedness

IB students develop intercultural understanding and respect, becoming global citizens who contribute to a more peaceful and sustainable world.

The ILM Academy Learner Profile

Inspired by the IB Learner Profile, ILM Academy celebrates attributes that describe our students as individuals and as responsible members of a global community.

Inquirers

We nurture our curiosity, developing skills for inquiry and research.

Knowledgeable

We develop and use conceptual understanding across a range of disciplines.

Thinkers

We use critical and creative thinking skills to analyze complex problems.

Communicators

We express ourselves confidently and creatively in more than one language.

Principled

We act with integrity and honesty, with a strong sense of fairness and justice.

Open-minded

We critically appreciate our own cultures and personal histories, as well as the values and traditions of others.

Caring

We show empathy, compassion and respect towards the needs and feelings of others.

Risk-Takers

We approach uncertainty with forethought and determination; we work independently and cooperatively.

Balanced

We understand the importance of balancing different aspects of our lives—intellectual, physical, and emotional.

Reflective

We thoughtfully consider the world and our own ideas and experience.
